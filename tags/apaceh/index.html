http로 접속했을 경우 http로 redirect하는 방법

19, 2018
apache 2.x 서버에서 http로 접속했을 경우 http로 redirect하는 방법 - 필요 모듈 : mod_rewrite - httpd.conf 설정 RewriteEngine On RewriteCond %{HTTPS} !=on RewriteRule ^(.*)$ https://%{HTTP_HOST}$1 [R,L] 예1. redirect https 직접 지정 (httpd.conf) <VirtualHost *:80> ServerName yourdomain.com Redirect permanent / https://yourdomain.com/ </VirtualHost> <VirtualHost _default_:443> ServerName yourdomain.com DocumentRoot /usr/local/apache2/htdocs SSLEngine On ... </VirtualHost> 예2. 모든 80 -> 443으로 지정 (httpd.conf) <VirtualHost *:80> ServerName yourdomain.com RewriteEngine On RewriteCond %{HTTPS} !=on RewriteRule ^(.*)$ https://%{HTTP_HOST}$1 [R,L] </VirtualHost> <VirtualHost _default_:443> ServerName yourdomain.
